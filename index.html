About Lindy

Lindy Ham is a South African artist, born in the Eastern Cape. After years in Johannesburg she returned to life on a farm — and the beauty that surrounds her there finds its way into every painting.

Specialising in watercolour and oil paintings, Lindy captures the essence of landscape through soft brushstrokes and vibrant colours, translating her love for the natural world into captivating artwork.

With over 50 original pieces created, each work tells a story of beauty, tranquillity, and artistic passion.
