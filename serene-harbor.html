Serene Harbor

Medium Watercolour on paper
Size A4 (210 × 297 mm)
Year 2026
Signature Hand-signed by the artist
Availability Available

Serene Harbor captures the tranquil beauty of a coastal landscape at dusk. The composition features a peaceful sailboat on calm waters, surrounded by a dreamy palette of purples, pinks, and blues. Distant trees frame the scene, creating a sense of depth and contemplation.

This watercolour reflects Lindy's deep connection with nature and her ability to convey emotion through soft brushstrokes and harmonious colour blending — a hallmark of her artistic style.
