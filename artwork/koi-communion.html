Koi Communion

Medium Watercolour on paper
Size A4 (210 × 297 mm)
Year 2026
Signature Hand-signed by the artist
Status Available

Koi Communion is a vibrant celebration of movement and grace in water. This watercolour features five beautiful koi in flowing water, their orange, white, and yellow patterns reflecting light as they swim in graceful harmony.

The layered watercolour technique creates depth and fluidity, showcasing Lindy's mastery of the medium. Each brushstroke conveys the natural beauty and serenity found in nature's quieter moments — a reflection of her South African farm upbringing.
